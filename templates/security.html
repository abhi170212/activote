Security

Protecting your vote and personal information

Election Security Overview

Our voting system employs multiple layers of security to ensure the integrity of elections and protect voter privacy. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of cybersecurity and election integrity.

Key Security Measures

  • End-to-End Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Multi-Factor Authentication: Enhanced security for election officials and administrators
  • Regular Security Audits: Independent third-party assessments of our systems
  • Voter Privacy Protection: Your personal information is never shared with candidates or parties
  • Secure Data Storage: All voter data is stored in encrypted, secure databases

Protecting Your Vote

Chain of Custody

Every ballot is tracked through a secure chain of custody from casting to counting. This ensures that votes are not altered or lost during the process.

Independent Verification

Our system allows for independent verification of election results through risk-limiting audits and manual recounts when necessary.

Post-Election Security

After elections, we conduct thorough security reviews and implement any necessary improvements for future elections.

Cybersecurity Measures

Network Security

Our systems are protected by enterprise-grade firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and continuous monitoring for suspicious activity.

Access Controls

Only authorized personnel have access to critical systems, and all access is logged and regularly reviewed.

Incident Response

We maintain a 24/7 incident response team ready to address any security concerns immediately.

Voter Education

Recognizing Disinformation

Learn to identify false information about voting procedures and candidates. Always verify information through official sources.

Reporting Security Concerns

If you notice any suspicious activity related to voting, report it immediately to election officials or through our secure reporting system.

Safe Voting Practices

  • Never share your voting credentials with anyone
  • Use secure, private devices and networks when possible
  • Verify you're on the official voting website
  • Report any technical issues immediately
