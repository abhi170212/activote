Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register to vote or use our services, we may collect:

  • Name, address, date of birth
  • Government-issued identification numbers
  • Contact information (email, phone number)
  • Voting history and participation records

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referring website addresses

How We Use Your Information

Election Administration

We use your information to:

  • Verify your eligibility to vote
  • Maintain accurate voter registration records
  • Notify you of upcoming elections and important dates
  • Prevent fraud and ensure election integrity

Communication

We may use your contact information to:

  • Send you important election updates
  • Provide information about polling locations
  • Notify you of changes to voting procedures

Information Sharing and Disclosure

With Your Consent

We may share your information with your consent or at your direction.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal processes.

Protection of Rights

We may disclose information to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or the public.

Aggregate Information

We may share aggregated, non-personally identifiable information with partners and the public.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorized disclosure, or access.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Your Rights

Access and Correction

You have the right to access and correct your personal information in our records.

Data Portability

You may request a copy of your personal information in a structured, machine-readable format.

Deletion

You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and regulatory requirements.

Objections

You may object to the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances.
